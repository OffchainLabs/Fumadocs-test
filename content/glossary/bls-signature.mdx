---
id: bls-signature
title: 'BLS Signature'
---

A cryptographic scheme that allows multiple signatures to be aggregated and compacted into one efficiently verifiable, constant-sized signature. Used in the [Arbitrum AnyTrust Protocol](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-anytrust-protocol) for the [Data Availability Committee (DAC)](/docs/glossary#data-availability-committee-dac)'s signatures.
