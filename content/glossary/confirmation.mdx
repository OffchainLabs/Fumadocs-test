---
id: confirmation
title: 'Confirmation'
---

The decision by an [Arbitrum chain](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-chain) to finalize an assertion as part of the chain's history. Once an [assertion](/docs/glossary#assertion) is confirmed its [Child-to-parent chain Messages](/docs/glossary#l2-to-l1-message) (e.g., withdrawals) can be executed.
