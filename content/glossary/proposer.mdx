---
id: proposer
title: 'Proposer'
---

A [Validator](/docs/glossary#validator) configured to actively submit new [Assertions](/docs/glossary#assertion) to the parent chain under [BoLD](/docs/glossary#bold). Proposers post the largest bonds in the staking hierarchy to deter delay attacks. Contrast with a [Watchtower Validator](/docs/glossary#watchtower-validator), which only observes, and a [Defensive Validator](/docs/glossary#defensive-validator), which acts only to counter an invalid claim.
