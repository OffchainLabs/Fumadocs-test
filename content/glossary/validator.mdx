---
id: validator
title: 'Validator'
---

An [Arbitrum Full Node](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-full-node) that tracks the status of the chains' [Assertion](/docs/glossary#assertion)s. A validator may be a [Watchtower Validator](/docs/glossary#watchtower-validator), a [Defensive Validator](/docs/glossary#defensive-validator), or an [Active Validator](/docs/glossary#active-validator).
