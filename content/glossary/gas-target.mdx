title: 'Gas target'
---

The target consumption rate for an Arbitrum chain. [Arbitrum One](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-one) and [Arbitrum Nova](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-nova). When gas usage exceeds this limit, fees rise. Read more in the [gas and fees article](/how-arbitrum-works/deep-dives/gas-and-fees.mdx).
