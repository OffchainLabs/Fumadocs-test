title: 'Custom gas token'
---

A non-**ETH** **ERC-20** token chosen at [Arbitrum chain](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-chain) deployment time to be used for paying transaction fees on that chain. Deposits work by escrowing the **ERC-20** in the chain's bridge on the [parent chain](/docs/glossary#parent-chain) and crediting the equivalent amount as the native asset on the child chain, where it replaces **ETH** everywhere **ETH** would otherwise be charged for gas.
