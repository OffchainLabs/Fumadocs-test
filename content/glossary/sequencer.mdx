---
id: sequencer
title: 'Sequencer'
---

An entity (currently a single-party on Arbitrum One) given rights to order transactions in the [Sequencer Inbox](/docs/glossary#sequencer-inbox) over a fixed window of time, who can thus give clients sub-blocktime [Soft Confirmations](/docs/glossary#soft-confirmation). (Not to be confused with a [Validator](/docs/glossary#validator)).
