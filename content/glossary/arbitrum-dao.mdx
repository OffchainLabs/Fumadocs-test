id: arbitrum-dao
title: 'Arbitrum DAO'
---

The onchain governance body that owns and operates [Arbitrum One](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-one) and [Arbitrum Nova](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-nova). Holders of the **ARB** token vote on protocol upgrades, treasury actions, and [Arbitrum Expansion Program](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-expansion-program) revenue distributions.
