title: 'Token Gateway'
---

A pair of contracts in the token bridge—one on the [Parent chain](/docs/glossary#parent-chain) , one on the [Child chain](/docs/glossary#child-chain)—that provide a particular mechanism for handling the transfer of tokens between layers. Token gateways currently active in the bridge include the [`StandardERC20` gateway](/docs/glossary#standarderc20-gateway) , the [Generic-Custom Gateway](/docs/glossary#genericcustom-gateway) , and the [**WETH** Gateway](/docs/glossary#weth-gateway).
