id: wasmer
title: 'WASMer'
---

A popular WebAssembly runtime for executing [WASM](/docs/glossary#wasm) binaries. [A fork of WASMer](https://github.com/OffchainLabs/wasmer) is used for executing [Stylus](/docs/glossary#stylus) programs. WASMer executes considerably faster than Geth executes EVM code, contributing to Stylus's lower fees.
