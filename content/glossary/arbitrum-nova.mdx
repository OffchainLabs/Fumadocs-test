title: 'Arbitrum Nova'
---

The first [Arbitrum AnyTrust Chain](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-anytrust-chain) running on Ethereum mainnet. Introduces cheaper transactions; great for gaming and social use-cases. Implements the [Arbitrum AnyTrust Protocol](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-anytrust-protocol), not the [Arbitrum Rollup Protocol](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-rollup-protocol) protocol. Governed by the [Arbitrum DAO](https://docs.arbitrum.foundation/gentle-intro-dao-governance).
