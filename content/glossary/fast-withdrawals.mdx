id: fast-withdrawals
title: 'Fast withdrawals'
---

A protocol-level withdrawal path on an [Arbitrum chain](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-chain) in which a configured validator committee attests to the withdrawn amount, allowing child-to-parent exits to settle in minutes rather than waiting out the full [challenge period](/docs/glossary#challenge-period). Distinct from a [fast-exit liquidity exit](/docs/glossary#fast-exit--liquidity-exit), which is a third-party liquidity provider rather than a protocol primitive.
