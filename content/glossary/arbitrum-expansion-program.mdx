title: 'Arbitrum Expansion Program (AEP)'
---

The licensing framework under which third parties can deploy new [Arbitrum chains](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-chain) outside [Arbitrum One](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-one) and [Arbitrum Nova](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-nova). In exchange for permissionless deployment, the chain remits 10% of net revenue—8% to the [Arbitrum DAO](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-dao) and 2% to ecosystem programs.
