The initial builders Arbitrum; current contributors to the Arbitrum ecosystem and service providers to the [Arbitrum DAO](https://docs.arbitrum.foundation/gentle-intro-dao-governance). Offchain also runs and maintains the [Sequencers](/docs/glossary#sequencer) for [Arbitrum One](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-one) and [Arbitrum Nova](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-nova).
