id: alt-da
title: 'Alt-DA'
---

An "alternative data-availability" mode for an [Arbitrum chain](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-chain) in which transaction data is posted to a third-party DA layer (for example, Avail or Celestia) rather than to Ethereum calldata/blobs (Rollup mode) or to a [DAC](/docs/glossary#data-availability-committee-dac) ([AnyTrust](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-anytrust-protocol) mode). Alt-DA exposes a different trust profile than either built-in mode and is configured at chain deployment.
