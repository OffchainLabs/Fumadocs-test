id: bonder
title: 'Bonder'
---

A [Validator](/docs/glossary#validator) who deposits a bond (in Ether on [Arbitrum One](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-one) and [Arbitrum Nova](/docs/glossary#arbitrum-nova) ) to vouch for a particular [assertion](/docs/glossary#assertion) in an Arbitrum Chain. A validator who bonds on a false assertion can expect to lose their bond. An honest bonder can recover their bond once the assertion they are bonded on has been confirmed.
_Also known as: staker_
